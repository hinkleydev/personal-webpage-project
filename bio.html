Bio

I'm based in the North of England, currently working at Barclays as a technology developer. My main interests outside of coding are cars and crochet, which really do not go together in the slightest, unless you're me and you're trying to crochet a tiny replica of your own car eventually.

While I don't have any crochet projects to show you just yet, I will tell you more about Maya, my pride and joy Mini One R56.

Maya is my third car, she's done about 52,000 miles in her lifetime and still looks fresh of the factory line. She's got a 1.6L engine, capable of outputting 98 brake horsepower. While I am tempted to take her to Oulton Park, I don't think my blackbox would like it very much.
